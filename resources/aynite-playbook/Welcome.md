# Welcome to Aynite! 👋

**A.Y.N.I.T.E — All You Need Is The Editor.**

Seriously. That's the whole idea.

Aynite is a desktop app that brings everything you need into one place — your files, your AI assistant, your tools, all inside a beautiful editor. No switching between apps, no copying and pasting, no complicated setup.

---

## The Big Picture

Aynite is built on three simple ideas:

- **Extremely easy.** If you can open a text editor, you can use Aynite. No complicated settings, no confusing dashboards. It's a clean, beautiful editor that just works — and the AI is always one keystroke away.

- **Extremely flexible.** Aynite can connect to almost anything on your computer. With **commands** (scripts that do specific tasks) and **skills** (AI-guided workflows that teach the assistant how to handle specialized jobs), you can make it do whatever you need.

- **Extremely integrable.** If something has an API or SDK, you can connect it to Aynite. You don't have to wait for the "official plugin" or some new feature. Want to work with Notion? Jira? Your own custom tool? Wire it up with a command or a skill and you're done.

> 🚧 **Aynite is under active development, and it's far from perfect.** But that's the direction. That's the ambition. And every release gets us closer.

## 🖱️ Explore What Aynite Can Do

The best way to see what Aynite can do is to **try it yourself**. Open the **File Browser** (on the left side of the app), find a `.json` file anywhere in your workspace, and click on it.

Aynite can turn JSON data into all sorts of visual things — charts, graphs, mind maps, flow diagrams, and more. Just click the **Data View** button after opening a JSON file and see what happens.

You can also:

- Open a **markdown (.md)** file and chat with the AI about it
- Open a **folder** in your workspace and browse your files with syntax highlighting


## 🤖 Step 1: Set Up Your AI Provider

Before the AI can help you, you need to tell it which model to use. Aynite supports many AI providers:

- **OpenAI** (GPT-4o, etc.)
- **Anthropic** (Claude)
- **Google** (Gemini)
- **DeepSeek**
- **Ollama** (Run models locally — completely free and offline!)
- And many more.

### How to configure

1. Open **Settings** from the app menu or sidebar.
2. Go to the **AI** tab.
3. Select your provider, enter your API key (if needed), and pick a model.
4. That's it. You're ready to chat.

> 💡 **Prefer local and free?** Set up [Ollama](https://ollama.ai), pull a model like `llama3.1`, and point Aynite to `http://localhost:11434`. No API key needed — everything stays on your computer.

## 🎨 Step 2: Create Your First Theme

Let's make something. You're going to create a custom theme using an AI **skill**.

1. Open the AI chat by pressing **`Ctrl+L`**.
2. Type something like:
   ```
   /create-theme make me a cozy autumn sunset theme
   ```
   (Try anything — "cyberpunk neon," "forest green," "ocean depths" — whatever you like.)
3. The AI will generate a complete theme file and save it to your themes folder.
4. Press **`Ctrl+R`** to refresh.
5. Go to **Settings → Appearance** and select your brand new theme.

Boom. You just created something. Feels good, right?
