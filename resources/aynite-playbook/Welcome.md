# Welcome to Aynite

Aynite is your advanced agentic AI editor, designed to empower your workflow with seamless AI integration.

## Getting Started

- **Explorer**: Use the left panel to navigate your project files.
- **AI Agent**: Use the right panel to interact with the AI agent. You can ask it to explain code, write new features, or debug issues.
- **Skills & Commands**: Extend Aynite's capabilities with custom skills and commands.

## Aynite Playbook

This folder contains a set of guides and examples to help you get the most out of Aynite. Feel free to explore and modify them!

Enjoy editing with Aynite!
